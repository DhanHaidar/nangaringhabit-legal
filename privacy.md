# Privacy Policy for Nangaring Habit

**Effective Date:** June 14, 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at **Nangaring Habit** ("Nangaring Studio", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This page is used to inform visitors and users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use our Service.

## 1. Information We Collect

Nangaring Habit is built with a strong **offline-first** architecture. This means your core app data—such as your habits, mood logs, schedules, and completion statistics—are stored **locally on your device**. 

We do not have access to, nor do we collect, store, or transmit your personal habit data to any external servers. Your data remains private to your device.

### Data Collected by Third-Party Services
While the app itself does not collect personal data directly to our servers, we use trusted third-party services to support certain features (such as advertising, analytics, and in-app purchases). These third parties may collect some information automatically:

*   **Google AdMob:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (like your Advertising ID), IP addresses, and interaction data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads and analyze ad performance.
*   **RevenueCat:** We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases (e.g., Nangaring Habit Pro). RevenueCat processes purchase history, your app user ID, and anonymized App Store/Play Store receipts. They do not have access to your credit card information; payments are processed securely by the app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store).

Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:
*   [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)
*   [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Since your personal habit data is kept locally on your device, we cannot and do not use it. 
The minimal information collected by third-party services is used strictly for:
*   Providing and maintaining app features (e.g., unlocking Pro features).
*   Serving relevant advertisements.
*   Understanding app usage and crash reports to improve the application.

## 3. Data Backup and Security

Your data is stored locally in a **Hive database** on your device. It is your responsibility to keep your device secure. 
If you use the built-in **JSON Backup/Export feature**, the file is saved directly to your device storage. We highly recommend keeping your backup files in a safe place.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your data, remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Since most of your personal data is stored locally, the security of that data also depends heavily on the physical and digital security of your device.

## 4. Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app we may collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

## 5. Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. This Service does not use these "cookies" explicitly. However, the app may use third-party code and libraries that use "cookies" to collect information and improve their services.
